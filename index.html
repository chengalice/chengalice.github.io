Squirrel Down

About

Welcome to Squirrel Cafe — the one and only place on Earth to enjoy a nutty roast with a nutty critter!

Our story began in 2016, when a curious little chipmunk wandered into our shop (back then it was just called "Cafe") and simply refused to leave. As time went by, this was becoming a real problem . . . until we came up with a crazy idea: why don't we make this a "thing"?

So, we rounded up a bunch of other squirrels from the neighbourhood (not as easy as it sounds!), integrated them into our cafe ecosystem (we'll spare you the details), and the rest, as we say, is HISQUIRY!
